404 - n. An error message indicating that an Internet address cannot be found. It is one of a variety of 4xx status codes, all of which describe errors resulting from a problem with the client. Other errors include:
  • 401Unauthorized
  • 403Forbidden
  • 410Gone
  • 412Precondition Failed
  • 418I'm a teapot
